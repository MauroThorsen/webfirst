software

    A series of computer data and instructions organized in a specific order is a non-tangible part of the computer.
The tangible part of the computer is called hardware and consists of the computer's outer casing and the parts and
circuits. Computer software needs to have hardware to operate, and vice versa, neither software nor hardware can actually
operate without cooperating.
